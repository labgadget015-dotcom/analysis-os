## Tips for Effective Email Communications

1. **Personalization**: Always use specific client names, metrics, and business context
2. **Clear CTAs**: End each email with a specific next step or question
3. **Metrics Matter**: Include hard numbers and projected impact when possible
4. **Transparency**: Be honest about challenges and realistic about timelines
5. **Professional but Warm**: Balance professionalism with genuine enthusiasm
6. **Timing**: Send emails Tuesday-Thursday, 9am-4pm for best open rates
7. **Length**: Keep email bodies to 150-250 words unless sharing detailed reports
8. **Formatting**: Use bullets, bold, and clear sections for readability
